About

I am currently studying Data Visualization and Analysis part time at the University of Minnesota. Thus far, I have completed units in Excel (including VBA), Python (including Pandas and Matplotlib), and SQL (including SQLAlchemy). We are currently working on our web visualization unit.

My background is in education and academia. I have taught for more than a decade and worked with students of all ages, from preschool to adult. I have experience working with native and non-native speakers (from novice to advanced levels). While I specialize in grammar and composition, I also have substantial experience teaching reading comprehension. The bulk of my recent experience has been in college preparation: SAT and ACT preparation and college admissions advising.

I hope to use the skills that I am aquiring in my current training to complement my background and current career by allowing me to make more data-based recommendations for my students.
